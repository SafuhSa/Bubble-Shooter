     Sinuous is a pure JavaScript game supported by HTML5 Canvas for pixel control, creating a detailed gameplay environment. Leverages object-oriented design, using inheritance to achieve DRY code!! it's lovely and easy to play. Use your mouse to guide your serpentine cursor safely through the field of red dots, snagging power ups to get various boosts and increase that all-powerful high-score.
